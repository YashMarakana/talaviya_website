Scrap

The metal recycling business is one of the biggest businesses globally. Globally the metal scrap industry is worth more than 500 billion dollars.

India’s scrap metal industry is booming with very good returns and at the same time it is very competitive. India has emerged the world’s second largest scrap importer.

Now Indian government is focusing more on metal and recycling industry and there is a good chance of good growth for the scrap industry in India.

We import scrap around the world including Canada, Singapore, Malaysia, Taiwan, Korea, Vietnam, Thailand, US, UK and various Middle East countries etc.
